How to use:

Select your country, then paste the URL of the seller's "detailed seller information" (see below), and then enter the number of pages you'd like to see. This has a hardcoded wait limit on both incoming and outgoing requests, so you cannot spam it, nonetheless I would appreciate if you didn't spam. In other words, if you enter 100 pages then you will wait at least 100 seconds for results.
